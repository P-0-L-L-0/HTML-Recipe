Pizza

Pizza is easy to make in the oven, just follow these steps!:

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese and Toppings

    1. Heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8.
    2. Place dough on baking tray BEFORE putting your choice of toppings on top.
    3. Put pizza in oven for 13min(+/-1min) till cooked through, enjoy!

      4. Yummy!
