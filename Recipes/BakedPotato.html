Baked Potatos

Baked potatos are easy to make in the oven, just follow these steps!:

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sour Cream

    1. Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7.
    2. Rub a little oil over each potato and put on the top shelf of the oven. Bake for 20 mins, then turn down the oven to 190C/170C fan/ gas 5 and bake for 45 mins-1 hr more until the skin is crisp and the inside soft.
    3. Make a cross in the centre of each potato, dollop over the soured cream and serve.

      4. Yummy!
