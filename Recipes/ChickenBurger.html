Chicken Burger

Chicken Burgers are easy to make in the air fryer, just follow these steps!:

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Flour
  • Salt and Seasoning
  • Brioche Bun
  • Burger Toppings

    1. Heat the air fryer to Chicken Mode.
    2. Cut the chicken breast in half horizonataly to make a flatter burger.
    3. Mix a half-cup of flour with plenty seasoning
    4. Coat the chicken in flour and put in air fryer on Chicken Mode, flipping over once half-way through the cooking time
    5. Serve immedietly on brioche bun with your choice of toppings

      6. Yummy!
